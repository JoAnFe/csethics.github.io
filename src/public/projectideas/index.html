Here are the slides for today’s class: Project Ideas [PDF].

Your first deliverable for the project is a Project Idea Proposal due Friday, 19 March (4:59pm). It should include:

  1. List of your team members
  2. Purpose of your project
  3. What you plan to do
  4. What you hope the outcome will be.

You can just send this to me by email - a short plaintext email is fine that answers these four questions is fine, but feel free to include anything else you want feedback on.

Please use this discussion post for posting ideas for projects and to find teammates to work with.
