Week 7 Summary / Next Project Deliverable

22 March 2021 by

The blog summary for week 7 is now posted: Week 7.

Thanks to Team 3 for writing the blog, and to Team 5 for leading!

I’ve also updated the Week 6 post on the projects, with more information on the next deliverable:

  • Project Progress Report due Wednesday, 7 April (4:59pm):

Update on your project and what you have done so far, and what you plan to do for the remainder. This should be sent as an email to me (evans@virginia.edu) with all of your teammates cc’d. It can be plain text in the email, or an attached PDF, containing at least this information:

  1. Title for your project
  2. How your purpose, plan, and hoped outcome have changed from the original proposal. For most projects, this should at least be refining your idea from the proposal; for some, you may have changed direction, and then should explain the reason for this and how things have changed.
  3. What you have done so far
  4. Your plan for completing the project - what you will do over the final weeks of the semester, and where you hope to get by the final deadline.

Week 6: Project Ideas

11 March 2021 by

Here are the slides for today’s class: Project Ideas [PDF].

Your first deliverable for the project is a Project Idea Proposal due Friday, 19 March (4:59pm). It should include:

  1. List of your team members
  2. Purpose of your project
  3. What you plan to do
  4. What you hope the outcome will be.

You can just send this to me by email - a short plaintext email is fine that answers these four questions is fine, but feel free to include anything else you want feedback on.

Please use this discussion post for posting ideas for projects and to find teammates to work with.
