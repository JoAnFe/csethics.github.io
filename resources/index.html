Other Classes

This sheet, collected by Casey Fisler summarize courses on tech ethics: Tech Ethics Curriculum. Her SIGSCE 2020 paper, What Do We Teach When We Teach Tech Ethics? A Syllabi Analysis provides an analysis of these courses.

Some courses that were particularly influential on me include:

  • Seny Kamara’s course at Brown University: Algorithms for the People

  • Phillip Rogaway’s course at UC Davis: Ethics in an Age of Technology (Reading List)

  • Steve Bellovin’s course at Columbia University: Computers and Society

  • Joi Ito and Jonathan Zittrain’s MIT course on The Ethics and Governance of AI: Syllabus/Readings, Playlist

  • Moritz Hardt’s UC Berkeley course: Fairness in Machine Learning

  • Brent Hecht’s Northwestern University course: Algorithms and Society

  • Alex Hanna’s UC Bekeley course: Data, Power, and Infrastructure
